About

Varun Mittal

Co-Founding Team

Varun drives the overall business strategy and execution at Fintrek. He has driven the development of our asset allocation algorithms and rule-based equity strategies. Varun started as a software engineer and went on to work in areas such as data analytics, credit research and fund management. Over 15 years of experience with organizations such as Kotak, Aditya Birla and Amex. Graduate of IIT Delhi and ISB Hyderabad


Narayanan Ravindranathan

Co-Founding Team

Narayanan drives all aspects of investment and related due-diligence at Fintrek. He has worked extensively in roles across institutional equity research, fund management and wealth management. As an equity strategist his views have driven asset allocation decisions for large family offices. Over 13 years of experience with organizations such as Mercer, Kotak and Spark Capital. CFA Charterholder from CFA Institute (USA) and a B.Tech from NIT, Surat


Chinmay Mohapatra

Co-Founding Team

Chinmay heads Business Development, Private Deals and Client Relations at Fintrek. Navigating investment portfolios for large families across market cycles with a superior portfolio hygiene and efficient tax structuring is his focus area. Has worked with several companies on fund raising and M&A transactions besides guiding allocations to PE and VC funds in clients’ portfolios. Over 13 years of Work experience with organizations such as Kotak, Stan-C, and ICICI.
